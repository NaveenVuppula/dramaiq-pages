Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

DramaIQ ("we", "our", "the app") is a movie and TV trivia game built by Naveen Vuppula. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

DramaIQ is designed to be offline-first. We do not operate servers, databases, or user accounts. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers.

All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob — displays interstitial advertisements between quiz rounds. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions (premium ad-free plan). RevenueCat processes purchase data through Apple App Store or Google Play. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

Free users see interstitial ads between quiz rounds, powered by Google AdMob. Premium subscribers do not see any ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Children's Privacy

DramaIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service, please contact us.

5. Data Retention

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app. We retain no data on any server.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
