Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Pick a cinema (Bollywood, Tollywood, Kollywood, or Hollywood), choose a difficulty level, and answer 10 trivia questions per round. After each answer, read the explanation and tap "Next" to continue.

Is the app free?

Yes! All quiz content across all cinemas and languages is completely free. The optional premium subscription ($2.99/month or $19.99/year) only removes ads — no content is locked behind a paywall.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. DramaIQ works fully offline. All questions are bundled with the app. An internet connection is only needed to purchase premium or restore purchases.

Can I change the language?

Yes. Each cinema defaults to its native language (Telugu for Tollywood, Hindi for Bollywood, Tamil for Kollywood, English for Hollywood). You can switch to English in Settings, or use the language toggle during gameplay.

Will I see the same questions again?

The app tracks which questions you've seen and prioritizes unseen ones. Once you've played through all questions in a cinema/difficulty, they will repeat in a shuffled order.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > DramaIQ
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > DramaIQ

How do I restore my premium purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.
